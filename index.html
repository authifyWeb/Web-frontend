What is authifyWeb

authifyWeb is a powerful social project aimed at enhancing online security. At its core, it provides free tools, empowering users to identify scams and impersonation fraud.

authifyWeb Tools

authifyWeb provides free online tools like authifyURL and authifyFame to protect users from online scams.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is authifyWeb?

authifyWeb is a social project to help internet users easily identify online frauds and impersonation scams.

It also helps brands, creators, influencers etc to protect their brand reputation.

Is authifyWeb free?

Absolutely! You can verify URLs on our site at no cost.

We rely on generous donations from users like you for support.

Is authifyWeb Safe?

Yes authifyWeb is completely safe and trustworthy.

We are completely transparent about how our code works and most of our codes are available on our Github Repository.

The data we use to verify is submitted directly by the brand or the celebrity themselves.

How can I support authifyWeb?

You can support us by donating to the cause or by spreading information about authifyWeb and it services. You can get started here.

You can create blog posts, video contents etc to create awareness among your community.
