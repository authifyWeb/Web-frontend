Get Involved & Contribute

authifyWeb is a free & open source project supported via donations from individuals, companies and other organizations. We have no external investors and we would like to keep it that way. It also helps us in keeping it free and accessible for all.


We need your help to fight scammers. Together, we're solving a real problem for a better future. We are grateful for the companies and the kind hearted individuals who make authifyWeb happen.

Spread The Word


You could help in making the internet safer in multiple ways.

  • Post about authifyWeb on social media sites.
  • Influence your company to list their sites with authifyWeb.
  • Be a community ambassador to spread news about authifyWeb and teach them how to use our services to verify websites and social profiles.
  • Help us test our sites for compatibility across different browsers with their latest versions.
  • Join our wonderful team of volunteers! Help us in bug fixing, testing and translating to various languages and make the websites more inclusive and accessible.
  • Help in building a community and growing them.
