Privacy Policy for authifyNow extensions


The common privacy policy for authifyWeb applies to authifyNow extensions in addition to the additional privacy policy.


Additional privacy policy for authifyNow extensions

The extension is completely private. We are in no way tracking you or profiling you. The only time the extension get access to the "URL" you visit is when you open the extension by clicking it or by using the shortcut key. Once the extension closes our permission is revoked. So be it Naughty or Nice, your website visits are always private from us.

When you open the extension, it'll send the current URL of the page you visit to our API, which returns the name of the organisation.
